Splitwise Cash Settlement Project
Splitwise can be used to simplify debts among friends / colleagues. It uses an algorithm which restructures debt within groups of friends. It does not change the total amount that anyone owes, but it makes it easier to pay people back by minimizing the total number of payments.

For example: say, Alice, Bob, and Charlie go on a trip. Alice owes Bob $20, and Bob owes Charlie $20. Rather than making two separate payments, Splitwise would tell Alice to pay $20 to Charlie directly, thereby minimizing the total number of payments being made. This ensures that people are paid back more quickly and efficiently.
